Ingredients

  • Cream cheese
  • Shreedded sharp cheddar cheese
  • Jalapeno peppers
  • Bacon

Directions

  1. Preheat oven to 400 degrees F
  2. Line baking sheet with aluminum foil
  3. Mix cream cheese and cheddar cheese
  4. Fill jalapeno with cheese mixture
  5. Wrap pepper with bacon
  6. Arrange peppers on baking sheet
  7. Bake until bacon is crispy, 25 to 35 minutes
